The reason as to why we decided on this project is that the drone
technology is expanding rapidly. Drones may be employed to do good
things, and also to attack. We would like to know how to preserve
people against these dangers.

What does your project do? Explain in
a simple way what your project does
and how it works.

In our project, we are simulating the attacks of drones with the help of
a computer. The system demonstrates the movement and attacking of
the target using drones. Students are able to observe the simulation
and try the various ways of detecting or stopping the drones.
